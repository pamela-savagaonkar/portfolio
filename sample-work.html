History of the Rose

Throughout the world, the rose has been a symbol of romance. The tradition of gifting a rose for Valentine's Day began in the Victorian Age. Today there are hundreds of rose variations.

Top Reasons People Love Roses

  1. Flower fragrance
  2. Color variations
  3. Easy to grow
  4. Long lasting bloom
  5. Flower meaning
