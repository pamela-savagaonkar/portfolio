Thank you for contacting us!

We are happy to help your child with their academics. I have included our rate information below, next steps, information about our tutoring services, and a brief video about our services.

Next steps

  • Could you please submit an enrollment form? This should take 2-3 minutes to fill out.
  • Optional New Family Remote Meeting: We offer a new student remote meeting so that we can best understand what you are looking for and meet your family. You can sign up for a new student remote meeting.

Our Mission

We pride ourselves in providing students with top-notch, personalized support. Parents are kept in the loop about what is being covered during the tutor sessions. We want to make sure any learning gaps are addressed so that students have a solid understanding of the material and can excel ahead. We help students become more confident in problem-solving, motivate them to learn time management, and enable them to learn study skills. Our ultimate goal is to create a positive learning environment for our students.

Rates

Our tutoring rates depend on working remote or in-person, and the tutor's experience level. For more info, you can check out our FAQs.

In-person or Remote Tutoring

We offer in-person or remote tutoring services. For in-person tutoring, your child meets with their tutor at your home and is available in select cities. We want sessions to be as convenient as possible for students and parents.

Cancel/Reschedule Policy

We don't charge for sessions canceled/rescheduled with advance notice of 48 hours or more.

If you prefer to discuss this over the phone, you can reach us at 425-202-5277. We look forward to working with your family!
